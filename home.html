Opportunity Logger

Click on "Create a new opp" to create a new opportunity or click on "Opportunities" to see all your previous one.

To update an opportunity, click on update and select the activity.

Be aware that, based on the activity some information are required:

Prospecting: Client, Business Contact, Business Unit

Meeting: Client, Business Contact, Business Unit, Comment

Pitch: Client, Business Contact, Business Unit, Business Line, Amount, Comment

Feasibility: Client, Business Contact, Business Unit, Business Line, Product, Category, Amount â‚¬, Brief (attachment), Presale Rep, Comment

Lost: Client, Business Contact, Business Unit, Comment, Lost Reason

Offer / Won: All information required

Clients Registry

Click on "Add company" to create a new company or click on "Clients Registry" to see all companies.

To update clients, click on update and select info or contacts.

Remember that you will have to specify at least one contact to file an opportunity, so make sure you add at least one person to your client registry.

Dashboard

A fully automated report with insights about all your opportunities.

Please allow 15/30 minutes for your freshly logged data to appear in the reports.
